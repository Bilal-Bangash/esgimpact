Overview

To ensure transparency and give you more control over your personal information, this privacy policy governs how we, ESG Impact Inc. and its subsidiaries and affiliates (all together, “ESGi” “we”, “our” or “us”) use, collect and store personal data we collect or receive from or about you (“you”) such as in the following use cases:

1. When you browse or visit our website, https://www.esgi.io/

2. When you or interact with the contact form

3. When you complete a survey/questionnaire

4. When you subscribe to our newsletter(s) / blog(s)

We greatly respect your privacy, which is why we make every effort to provide a platform that would live up to the highest of user privacy standards. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully, so you can fully understand our practices in relation to personal data. “Personal Data” or “Personal Information” means any information that can be used, alone or together with other data, to uniquely identify any living human being.

This Privacy Policy will be updated as our internet services and solutions continue to grow. We ask you to check for the latest version at: https://www.esgi.io/privacy-policy/ If we implement a significant change to the way we store or use your personal data, we will post a notice on our Website or email you with a set the changes.

Data Collection

Currently, we collect do not collect cookies, log files, or other analytics tools. We only collect data when you request more information in our contact form as follows: data we collect, Full name, Email address, and Company name; why we collect it, to allow us to collect your request.

Data Protection

We have implemented appropriate technical, organizational and security measures designed to protect your personal data. However, please note that we cannot guarantee that the information will not be compromised because of unauthorized penetration to our servers or your personal device.

Data Retention

We will store your personal data until we no longer need the information and proactively delete it or you send a valid deletion request.

For further information, or if you have any questions, concerns or complaints regarding our compliance with this notice and the data protection laws, or if you wish to exercise your rights, we encourage you to first contact us at contact@esgi.io.
