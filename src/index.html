Impact Rating

Our customized assessment tracks ESG metrics & provides a risk mitigation analysis based on your sector operations.

To avoid “greenwashing” and make impactful next steps, our ESGi analyst supports your company with engaging discussions and impactful next steps.

M&A Deliverables
